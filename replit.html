How is Replit more than a simple web IDE?

Disclamer : I started working at Replit 4 months ago. My opinions here are my own.

I often see online comments about how Replit is just a web IDE. I thought I'd write this post to explain in what way exactly Replit is more than that.

On Replit, localhost === prod!

When you create a flask app or an express server on Replit you don't have to waste precious time setting up hosting because your dev environment is already deployed and can be accessed by a link. This merging between localhost and production is great for beginners or people wanting to quickly get a business idea up and running.

With every repl backed by Nix, Replit supports an arbitrary number of programming languages

Now that Replit uses Nix you can bring any programming language to Replit and not just that. Someone made a repl with Postgres in it. Also you have access to a lot of linux packages and utils.

Templates!

Users can make and use templates made by others. Want to make a FastAPI backend? there is a template for that. Want to make something in Django? There is also a template for that.

Templates are not limited to frameworks or programming languages someone could make a blog template to allow people to easily make their own blog or an ecommerce templates or a photo galery template, etc... The possibilities are almost endless.

You can share your repls with other replers
